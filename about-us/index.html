About Us

We manufacture natural perfumes and non-perfume products that are beneficial, healthy, and non-toxic. Our current range includes agarwood oil, natural attars, and traditionally brewed raw vinegar.

Agarwood is also known as Oud, Oudh, or Gaharu, and is among the most valued natural fragrance ingredients in the world.

What We Do

We focus on producing pure, natural products using traditional methods refined within our family over generations. Our approach emphasizes authenticity, quality, and long-term health benefits.

Our Location

We originate from Assam in eastern India, a region where agarwood trees grow naturally. The finest quality Indian Oud is sourced from this region.

Since January 2021, some members of our family have also settled in Gujarat in western India, which now serves as a hub for marketing and vinegar production.

A Family-Owned Business

We are a small, family-run business rooted in Assam, India.

By the grace of the Almighty, we strive to remain honest and truthful in all that we do. We see humanity as one family and do not discriminate based on country, region, language, ethnicity, race, religion, or colour.
